Types of Internet Addiction

Social Media Addiction

Compulsive scrolling and validation seeking behavior.

Gaming Addiction

Excessive gaming affecting sleep and responsibilities.

Online Shopping

Impulse buying and compulsive spending habits.

Online Relationship Dependency

Emotional reliance on digital interactions.

Signs & Symptoms

  • Preoccupation with online activities
  • Withdrawal when offline
  • Sleep disruption
  • Reduced productivity
  • Mood swings linked to screen use

Treatment & Recovery

Recovery strategies include cognitive behavioral therapy, digital detox planning, structured screen limits, and professional counseling support.
