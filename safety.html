Social Media Safety

Protect your identity, privacy, and emotional well-being online.

This module teaches secure social media habits, phishing awareness, privacy protection, and responsible communication.

Safety indicator

Golden rules

  • Think before posting personal details.
  • Never share passwords, OTPs, or recovery codes.
  • Verify information before forwarding it.

Healthy online behavior

  • Use respectful communication.
  • Mute or block harassment quickly.
  • Limit comparison-heavy feeds that affect mood.
