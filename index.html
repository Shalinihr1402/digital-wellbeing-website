Why Digital Well-Being Matters?

Excessive screen exposure impacts mental health, sleep cycles, concentration, and productivity. This system provides structured guidance for balanced digital behavior.

Quick Digital Wellness Tips

  • Follow 20-20-20 eye rule
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before sleep
  • Limit social media scrolling
  • Turn off non-essential notifications
  • Practice weekly digital detox
